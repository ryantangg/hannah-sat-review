1. D — factored form shows x-intercepts
2. A — vertex x = (2+(−4))/2 = −1, d = a(−3)(3) = −9a
3. A — parabola must cross y=3 twice
4. A — use quadratic formula or complete the square
5. 11 — plug in (3,6): 6 = 27 − 3b + 12
6. A — 13% decay = 87% retained = 0.87
7. D — 10% loss = 0.9 retained, every 20 yrs = t/20
8. 1.02 — 2% growth = multiply by 1.02
9. A — parallel lines: k/4 = 3/5, k = 12/5
10. 1/4 — proportional: a/2 = b/8 = 12/60
11. A — r² = (4/3)² + 1² = 25/9
12. A — complete the square: (x+2)²+(y−1)²=4
13. A — 8x/2y = 23x/2y = 23x−y = 212
14. A — a²−b² = 16, (a+b)(a−b)=16, 2(a−b)=16
15. C — light tubes are an alternative to windows
16. B — staffing cuts are a consequence of funding cuts
17. B — contrasts with what otters do (however)
18. C — adds another supporting fact (moreover)
19. D — participial phrase needs comma, not semicolon
